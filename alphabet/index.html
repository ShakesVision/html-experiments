How to use

  • Choose which languages to include by tapping the chips on the left.
  • Paste your custom letters (one per line) and press Load — or use defaults.
  • Upload audio files (optional). Name files exactly as the letter to map automatically, or map manually using the mapping screen.
  • Press Start. The app will show a letter, wait for the reveal delay, then play your audio and show the correct answer.
  • Use Infinite mode for practice, or Time-bound for quick rounds. Score is tracked on the top right.
